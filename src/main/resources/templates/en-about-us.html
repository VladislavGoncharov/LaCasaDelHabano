about

Cigar places and tasting areas in the centre of st. Petersburg

We are part of a global franchise selling Habanos. In our shops you can find the widest range of brands, and excellent storage conditions in optimal temperature and humidity guarantee the quality of our products.
The House of Cuban Cigars has exclusive vitolas such as Partagas Salomones, H. Upmann Sir Winston, the Limitada range and much more that many other establishments are unlikely to boast.
